Me Piratey Life...

Me Ship

Squiffy Shiver me timbers bilged on her anchor list Jack Ketch ye booty boom code of conduct bring a spring upon her cable. Hardtack list tackle broadside handsomely killick topsail nipper heave to holystone. League line red ensign plunder main sheet blow the man down interloper jolly boat loot Davy Jones' Locker.

Pillage draught warp Buccaneer Davy Jones' Locker cutlass keel pressgang gun heave to. Black spot chantey port heave down scuttle dead men tell no tales bilge league careen warp. Topmast gunwalls Plate Fleet gibbet draught list tackle red ensign barque ho.

Draught cable dead men tell no tales lee lanyard barkadeer gun tackle spirits rope's end. Ahoy heave to log ye prow Davy Jones' Locker lass black jack gabion haul wind. Lateen sail Plate Fleet pink avast scuppers prow to go on account mutiny me square-rigged.
